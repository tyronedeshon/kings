About Us

K•I•N•G•S equips youth and families with practical trade skills, mentorship, and community‑first leadership. Through programs in general maintenance, welding, music production, and violence prevention, we turn curiosity into capability and confidence.

What We Do

  • Hands‑on learning in skilled trades
  • Mentorship and career exposure
  • Community events at Kings Park

Programs & Services

General Maintenance

Safety, tools, drywall repair, TV mounting, home basics.

Music Production

Beat‑making, recording, creative expression.

Welding

Foundations of safety, tools, and practice.

Violence Prevention

Conflict resolution, mentorship, positive pathways.
