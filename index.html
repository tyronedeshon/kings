Donate a Vehicle — CARS (Careasy)

Thank you for supporting K.I.N.G.S by donating a vehicle. CARS (Careasy) handles pickup, paperwork, and title transfer. Follow the steps below to complete your donation.

How to donate (quick)

  1. Click the big Donate button below (opens Careasy/CARS page in a new tab).
  2. Complete the online donation form and choose K.I.N.G.S as the beneficiary.
  3. Schedule the pickup time — CARS coordinates with you.
  4. Receive a tax receipt via email (if eligible).

Donate Your Vehicle Now

Need help? Contact us at info@kingsorg.org and we'll assist you through the process.

What CARS handles

  • Title and paperwork
  • Pickup scheduling and transport
  • Issuing tax receipts when applicable
Why donate a vehicle?

Vehicles are one of the highest-impact gifts — they can be sold to fund programs, provide equipment, or cover transport costs for our initiatives.

About K.I.N.G.S Organization

K.I.N.G.S — Key Innovators for New Growth in Society — is a nonprofit focused on youth empowerment, hands-on trade skills, and community events that increase opportunity and connection in Detroit neighborhoods.

We provide affordable handyman and automotive services, host free events, and run educational programs to build generational wealth and life skills.
